\[
\Delta P = f \cdot \frac{L}{D} \cdot \frac{\rho v^2}{2}
\]

- \(\Delta P\) = pressure loss
- \(f\) = friction factor
- \(L\) = length of the pipe (10 m)
- \(D\) = diameter of the pipe (0.1 m)
- \(\rho\) = density of water (approximately 1000 kg/m³)
- \(v\) = velocity of water (0.01 m/s)

\[
Re = \frac{\rho v D}{\mu}
\]

Given that the velocity is low, we might expect laminar flow, where \(f\) can be calculated as:
