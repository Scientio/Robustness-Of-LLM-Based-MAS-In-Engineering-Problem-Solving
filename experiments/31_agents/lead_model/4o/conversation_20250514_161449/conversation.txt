\[
\Delta P = f \cdot \frac{L}{D} \cdot \frac{\rho v^2}{2}
\]

Where:

The friction factor \( f \) depends on whether the flow is laminar or turbulent. \[

Since \( \text{Re} < 2000 \), the flow is laminar, and \( f = \frac{64}{\text{Re}} = 64 \).
