\[ \Delta P = f \cdot \frac{L}{D} \cdot \frac{\rho \cdot v^2}{2} \]

Given the low velocity, the flow is likely laminar (\( Re < 2000 \)), and for laminar flow, \( f = \frac{64}{Re} \).
