- \(f\) is the friction factor,
- \(L\) is the length of the pipe,

- \(v\) is the flow velocity (0.01 m/s).

\[

\]

\[
Re = \frac{\rho v D}{\mu}
\]

Assuming the dynamic viscosity \(\mu\) of water is approximately \(0.001 \, \text{Pa} \cdot \text{s}\).
