\[ h_f = f \cdot \frac{L}{D} \cdot \frac{v^2}{2g} \]

- \( h_f \) is the head loss,
- \( f \) is the friction factor,
- \( L \) is the length of the pipe (10m),
- \( D \) is the diameter of the pipe (0.1m),
- \( v \) is the flow velocity (0.01m/s), and
- \( g \) is the acceleration due to gravity (approximately 9.81 m/sÂ²).

\[ Re = \frac{\rho v D}{\mu} \]
