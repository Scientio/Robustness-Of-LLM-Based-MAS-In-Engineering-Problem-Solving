\[
\Delta P = f \cdot \frac{L}{D} \cdot \frac{\rho v^2}{2}
\]

- \(f\) is the friction factor,

- \(D\) is the diameter of the pipe (0.1m),

- \(v\) is the flow velocity (0.01 m/s).

\[

Next, we can use the appropriate correlation for the friction factor based on whether the flow is laminar or turbulent. Given the low velocity, we might expect laminar flow, which gives us:

\[
