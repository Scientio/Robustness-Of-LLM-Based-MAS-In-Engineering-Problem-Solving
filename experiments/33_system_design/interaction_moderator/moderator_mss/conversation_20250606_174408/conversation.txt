3. **Diameter of the pipe (D)**: 0.1 m
4. **Dynamic viscosity of water (μ)**: 0.001 Pa·s

Substituting these values into the Reynolds number formula:

\[ Re = \frac{ρ \cdot v \cdot D}{μ} = \frac{1000 \cdot 0.01 \cdot 0.1}{0.001} = 1000 \]

\[ ΔP = f \cdot \frac{L}{D} \cdot \frac{ρ \cdot v^2}{2} \]
