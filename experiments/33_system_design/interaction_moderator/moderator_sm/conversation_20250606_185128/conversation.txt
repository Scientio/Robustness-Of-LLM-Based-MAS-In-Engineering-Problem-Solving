- \(v\) is the flow velocity.

First, we need to find the Reynolds number (\(Re\)) to determine the friction factor (\(f\)). The Reynolds number is calculated as:

\[
Re = \frac{\rho v D}{\mu}
\]

where \(\mu\) is the dynamic viscosity of water (approximately \(1 \times 10^{-3} \, \text{Pa·s}\) at room temperature).

Given the values:
